Junyan Su

School of Data Science, City University of Hong Kong.

This is Junyan Su (苏隽岩). I received my bachelor degree in Computer Science and Technology from ShanghaiTech University in 2019. During my stay at ShanghaiTech, I joined Prof. Boris Houska’s group. From 2018 to 2019, I spent my senior year as a visiting student in the Department of EECS at UC Berkeley.

Currently, I am a Ph.D. candidate at School of Data Science, City University of Hong Kong in Prof. Minghua Chen’s group. My current research interests include intelligent transportation, and online optimization.
